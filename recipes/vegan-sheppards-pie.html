Vegan Sheppards Pie

Description

Vegan shepherd's pie is a delicious plant-based takes on the classic comfort dish, with a savory filling of veggies, legumes, and vegan protein, topped by a layer of creamy mashed potatoes. This cruelty-free version provides the same hearty and flavorful experience without any animal products.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups lentils (green or brown), cooked
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce or tamari
  • 1 teaspoon thyme, dried
  • 1 teaspoon rosemary, dried
  • 1 teaspoon oregano, dried
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegan butter
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
