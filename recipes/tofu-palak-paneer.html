Tofu Palak Paneer

Description

Vegan tofu palak paneer is a plant-based version of the traditional Indian dish, blending tofu cubes in a creamy spinach and cashew curry for a flavorful twist on this classic recipe. It's a delicious and cruelty-free alternative to the traditional paneer version.

Ingredients

  • 1 block extra-firm tofu, cubed
  • 4 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup cashews, soaked
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)
